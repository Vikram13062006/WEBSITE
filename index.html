About us

KL University, formerly Koneru Lakshmaiah College of Engineering (KLCE) and Koneru Lakshmaiah University, is a higher educational institution Deemed to be University, located in Vaddeswaram, near Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1980 as a college of engineering, it consists of eight schools, offering academic programs at UG, PG, doctoral, and post-doctoral industry-focused courses.

Planning And Development - Studios

Audio-Visual Studios are specialized facilities equipped for creating, editing, and producing multimedia content, including audio and video elements. These studios are commonly used in film, television, radio, music production, advertising, and other media industries.

They are designed to support a variety of tasks such as:

  • Audio Production: Recording voiceovers, music, sound effects, and mixing audio tracks.
  • Video Production: Filming, editing, and producing video content, including films, commercials, and TV shows.
  • Post-Production: Editing and enhancing both audio and video through processes like sound design, color grading, visual effects, and final mixing.
  • Broadcasting: Some studios are set up to support live broadcasting, including live events, news shows, and podcasts.
